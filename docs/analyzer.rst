The NexusAnalyzer
=================

The Core Execution Manifold
---------------------------

The ``NexusAnalyzer`` is the absolute core of the Nexus library. Modeled directly after ``scikit-learn``, it standardizes the computation of massive mathematical risk matrices into three simple methods: ``.calibrate()``, ``.compute()``, and ``.fetch()``.
